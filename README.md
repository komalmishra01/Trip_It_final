# TripIt

[![Flutter](https://img.shields.io/badge/Flutter-2.20-blue?logo=flutter&logoColor=white)](https://flutter.dev/)

**TripIt** is a Flutter-based travel planning and booking app designed to simplify your trips. It allows users to explore destinations, plan itineraries, and manage travel details efficiently.  

---

## App Description

TripIt is built for travelers who want a smooth, organized, and enjoyable experience. With TripIt, users can browse destinations, view detailed information, save their favorite places, and create personalized trip plans.  

The app integrates maps for location-based services and ensures that essential travel information is always accessible. Whether traveling solo or with family, TripIt keeps your trips organized and stress-free.  

---

## Features

- **Explore Destinations:** Browse popular travel locations with detailed descriptions.  
- **Trip Planning:** Create and customize itineraries for single or multiple days.  
- **Favorites:** Save destinations for quick access during trip planning.  
- **Detailed Info:** Access key details about each destination, including highlights and timings.  
- **Smooth Navigation:** User-friendly and intuitive interface for seamless app experience.  
- **Map Integration:** Find routes and nearby attractions easily.  
- **Offline Support:** Access previously viewed information without internet (optional).  

---

## How It Works

1. Open the app and browse popular destinations.  
2. View details and save favorites.  
3. Create a trip plan by selecting destinations and arranging the order of visits.  
4. Navigate the map to track your journey and nearby attractions.  
5. Review and update your trip itinerary as needed.
